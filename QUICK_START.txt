================================================================================
                    CHASED - QUICK START GUIDE
================================================================================

3 STEPS TO PLAY:

1. BUILD CITY
   Unity Menu → Chased → Quick City Setup
   Click "BUILD COMPLETE CHASE CITY"

2. OPEN SCENE
   Assets/Scenes/chased.unity

3. PRESS PLAY
   Click ► button at top of Unity


CONTROLS:
---------
WASD or Arrow Keys = Drive
Space = Brake


GOAL:
-----
Escape the cop cars chasing you!
Use the city streets and intersections to lose them.


TIPS:
-----
• Turn sharply at intersections to shake cops
• Don't crash into buildings
• Higher speed = harder to turn
• Cop cars are aggressive - they'll ram you!


NEED MORE HELP?
---------------
See HOW_TO_PLAY.txt for detailed instructions
See CITY_BUILDER_GUIDE.txt for city building options


ADJUSTING DIFFICULTY:
---------------------
Before building city, in Quick City Setup window:
- More cops = harder (2-12 cops available)
- Bigger city = more escape routes
- Smaller city = more intense chases

To change cop speed/aggression:
- Select any CopCar in scene
- Adjust AIDriverInput component settings


TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
Q: Can't control car?
A: Make sure PlayerCar exists in scene, check Input System is installed

Q: Cops don't chase?
A: Check GameManager exists, verify AIDriverInput not disabled

Q: No buildings?
A: Click "Load Building Prefabs" buttons first, then "Build City"
